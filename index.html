Welcome

The Law Office of Bruce Hudson Towne provides exceptional legal representation in the areas of estate planning, probate administration and litigation, trust administration and litigation, conservatorships, and financial elder abuse litigation for clients in Carmichael and throughout Sacramento, Placer, and El Dorado Counties. For further information on our areas of expertise, please see our practice area overview.

Our attorneys have five decades of collective legal experience, and practice exclusively in estate planning, probate, and trust law. Firm principal Bruce Hudson Towne is a Certified Specialist in Estate Planning, Trust and Probate Law, as designated by the State Bar of California Board of Legal Specialization. As a testament to our reputation in the legal community, many of our clients are referred to us by other attorneys, who know of our ability to craft creative solutions to complex problems.

At the Law Offices of Bruce Hudson Towne, we are committed to helping our clients improve their situation and providing them with peace of mind. We welcome your inquiry regarding your trust and estate matter. To schedule a consultation with one of our attorneys, please contact the Law Offices of Bruce Hudson Towne.
